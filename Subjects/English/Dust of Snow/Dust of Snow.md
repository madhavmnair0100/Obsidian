## Dust of Snow

> [!note] Summary
> The poet was in a depressive mood. He was sitting under a **hemlock tree**. When the crow sat on the tree and shook off the 'dust of snow' on the poet, his mood suddenly changed from sad to happy.

> [!question] Explain the basis of the poem 'Dust of Snow'.
> The poet was sitting helpless, soaked in thoughts thinking about his problems and wasting away his crucial time and energy in unnecessary and futile thinking. The crow's gesture bestowed upon him <mark style="background:rgba(0, 255, 0, 0.2)">optimism</mark>. He decided to shake off his problems and think of finding a solution instead of regretting and weeping. The poet conveys the message that there is nothing good or bad in life. **Negative thinking** is solely responsible for our sorrow. Instead of grieving over a bad situation, we should thrive forward to sought problems successfully.
